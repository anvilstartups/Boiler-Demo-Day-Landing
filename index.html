Our Startups

These are some of the best startups coming out of Purdue University and will be the ones pitching at Demo Day!

Dime is creating a one-stop shop where brands looking for short-term alternatives to a storefront can host pop-up shops that enable them to interact directly with desired consumer groups.

FlykeART is a smart airplane food cart that connects to the cabin via Wi-Fi. This allows airlines to reduce operating costs and optimize the catering process.

Vastra plans to combat the problem of sleeping at the wheel by building next generation non-invasive driver safety systems.

GetSpace connects students who are looking for short-term accommodation during their internships or co-ops.

Make A Thing is an online design community with simplified modeling software in order to reduce the barriers to entry for design.
